OUR MISSION
Together We Can Change The World
We recognize that it’s not always possible for organizations to secure the support of influencers to champion their cause. Cloutvocate offers an in-depth platform that strategically aligns partners with similar goals and interests to establish mutually beneficial partnerships between nonprofit organizations, corporations, and influencers. Our goal is to identify and match the perfect partnerships using new technology to maximize exposure and reach, augment mission awareness, boost fundraising goals and donations, as well as incentivize influencers for their help in promoting purposeful causes.
